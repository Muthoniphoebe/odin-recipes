Hearty Homemade Lasagna

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with our Hearty Homemade Lasagna recipe. This classic Italian dish features layers of tender lasagna noodles, rich and flavorful meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and a gooey mozzarella topping. Each bite offers a delightful combination of savory beef, aromatic herbs, and a touch of garlic, perfectly balanced with the creaminess of the cheese. Whether you're preparing a family dinner or hosting a gathering, this lasagna is sure to impress and satisfy everyone's taste buds. Serve with a side of garlic bread and a fresh salad for a complete, mouth-watering meal.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6-ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (15-ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

For the Cheese Filling:

  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil

For the Lasagna:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Optional:

  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

Step 1:Prepare the Meat Sauce:

In a large skillet or saucepan, cook the ground beef and Italian sausage over medium-high heat until browned and crumbled.

Add chopped onions and minced garlic, cooking until onions are translucent and fragrant.

Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.

Simmer uncovered for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in chopped parsley towards the end. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 2:Prepare the Cheese Filling:

In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, salt, dried parsley, and dried basil. Mix until smooth and well blended. Set aside.

Step 3:Cook the Lasagna Noodles:

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.

Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente.

Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking. Set aside.

Step 4:Assemble the Lasagna:

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.

Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce, slightly overlapping.

Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture evenly over the noodles.

Sprinkle with a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.

Repeat layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and cheeses.

Finish with a final layer of noodles topped with remaining meat sauce and cheeses.

Step 5: Bake the Lasagna:

Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, tenting slightly to prevent cheese from sticking.

Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes.

Remove foil and bake uncovered for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until hot and bubbly and cheese is melted and golden brown on top.

Step 6:Let it Rest and Serve:

Remove from oven and let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.

Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.

Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
