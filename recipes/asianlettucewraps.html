Asian Lettuce Wraps

Delight in the fresh and savory flavors of our Asian Lettuce Wraps. This dish features tender, seasoned ground chicken or turkey mixed with crunchy water chestnuts and flavorful Asian sauces, all wrapped in crisp lettuce leaves. Each bite offers a perfect balance of textures and tastes, complemented by a medley of aromatic herbs and a zesty dipping sauce. Whether served as an appetizer or a light main course, these wraps are sure to satisfy your cravings for delicious Asian-inspired cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken or turkey
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 (8-ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and finely chopped
  • 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 head iceberg or butter lettuce, leaves separated

Preparation Steps

1. Cook the Filling:

  • Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and minced ginger. Cook until onions are soft and translucent, about 2-3 minutes.
  • Add ground chicken or turkey to the skillet, breaking it up with a spoon. Cook until meat is browned and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes.
  • Stir in chopped water chestnuts, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and sriracha sauce (if using). Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until heated through and flavors are combined.

2. Assemble the Wraps:

  • Spoon a portion of the filling into the center of each lettuce leaf.
  • Fold the sides of the lettuce leaf over the filling, then roll it up from the bottom, creating a wrap.

3.

  • Arrange the lettuce wraps on a serving platter.
  • Serve with additional soy sauce, hoisin sauce, or sriracha sauce on the side for dipping.
  • Garnish with chopped green onions, cilantro, or sesame seeds if desired.
  • Enjoy your Asian Lettuce Wraps while they're fresh and flavorful!
